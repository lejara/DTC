Custom Words

Add in your own words separated by :: and the image by >

- You can mix in a word with, or without an image link
- Each word has a max character limit of 45
- Image links must be the direct link of the image

Adds and imports a channel's BTTV emotes into your custom words list.

Adds and imports a channel's FFZ emotes into your custom words list.

How To Play

1. Make sure to allow pop ups, if prompted!

2. Enter your channel name, then press "Start Game"

3. "Start Game" button will change to "Next Word" when you start.

Side Notes

- Chat's guesses must be at the beginning of their message.

- You don't have to reset the game if you change any of the settings, except the channel name.

- If your chat is just randomly guessing, putting your chat in slow mode can help.

- Officially supports Firefox and Google Chrome only!
